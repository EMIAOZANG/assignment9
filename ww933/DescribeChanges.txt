In general, from 2009 to 2012, the income per person in Asia and South America increased steadily, while the income
barely increased in Africa and North America. In the meanwhile, the income per person increased steadily in Europe from
2009 to 2011, and then dropped in 2012.

Besides, the differences of income per person in North America is getting smaller from year to year. I.e. the income
per person is more balanced from 2009 to 2012 in North America. However, the same situation doesn't happened to other places.
